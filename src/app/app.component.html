WHAT WE DO

Multi-track your full band or solo project. Drums, bass, guitar, vocals, keyboard, strings, auxiliary percussion, and any type of sound you could ever think of creating. We also offer live band tracking for capturing a specific experience you wish to convey to your audience.

Recording an audiobook? Narrating your latest nature documentary? Need to record content for your latest mindfulness app? We've got you covered with high fidelity condenser and dynamic microphones along with top of the line sound isolation to give you the cleanest possible tracks for your project.

Record your latest, cutting edge podcast with up to eight of your friends or colleagues. We'll capture all voices clearly and independently for a clear and concise mix to maximize listenability.
